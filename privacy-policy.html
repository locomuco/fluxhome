Privacy Policy

App: Flux Home  ·  Last updated: April 17, 2025

Flux Home is a Matter smart-home controller app for Android. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Flux Home does not send any information to external servers, analytics services, or third parties. All data the app uses stays on your device.

2. Permissions used

Camera

The camera is used exclusively to scan QR codes printed on Matter device packaging during the commissioning (setup) process. Camera images are processed entirely on-device in real time and are never stored, transmitted, or shared.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth (BLE) is used to connect to nearby Matter devices during commissioning. No Bluetooth data is stored beyond the session or shared with any third party.

Location (approximate)

Android requires location permission to scan for nearby Wi-Fi networks. This scan is performed only when you choose to commission a Wi-Fi–capable device and need to select a network. No location data is stored or transmitted.

Local network

The app communicates directly with your Matter devices over your local network (Thread or Wi-Fi). No traffic is routed through external servers.

3. Local storage

The app stores a small amount of data in your device's local storage (Android SharedPreferences), including:

  • The list of commissioned devices (name, type, network details)
  • Thread dataset credentials you have saved in the app
  • Last-known device states (on/off, brightness, temperature)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

4. Third-party services

Flux Home does not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or social login providers.

5. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "last updated" date.
