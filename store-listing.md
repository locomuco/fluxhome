# Flux Home — Play Store Listing

## Short description

Local Matter smart home control. No cloud. No subscriptions. No lock-in.

## Long description

Flux Home puts you in full control of your smart home — no cloud required.

Most smart home apps phone home. Every tap you make travels to a server, gets processed, and comes back. That means your lights depend on someone else's uptime, your data sits on someone else's infrastructure, and the day that company shuts down or changes their terms, your devices stop working.

Flux Home is different. Commands go directly from your phone to your device — over your local network, every time. No internet required. No accounts. No subscriptions.

**True local control**
Every command — toggle a light, adjust a thermostat, change brightness — is sent directly to the device over your local Thread or Wi-Fi network. There is no cloud relay, no intermediary server, and no latency spike when your internet is down.

**Ecosystem independent**
Flux Home is built on Matter, the open smart home standard supported by the industry's major platforms and hundreds of device manufacturers. If a device is Matter-compatible, Flux Home can commission and control it — regardless of the brand, regardless of what other apps it supports.

**Commission with a QR scan**
Adding a new device takes seconds. Open the scanner, point it at the QR code on the device or its packaging, and Flux Home handles the rest — BLE pairing, network credentials, fabric commissioning.

**Your data stays on your phone**
Flux Home stores your device list and settings locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded. No analytics. No crash reporters. No third-party SDKs watching your usage.

**What Flux Home supports**
- Lights and dimmers (on/off, brightness)
- Thermostats (heating and cooling setpoints)
- Sensors (contact, occupancy, temperature, humidity, air quality)
- Fans
- Thread and Wi-Fi devices
- OTA firmware updates for supported devices

**Requirements**
- Android 8.1 or later
- A Matter-compatible device
- A Thread Border Router or Wi-Fi network for your devices
